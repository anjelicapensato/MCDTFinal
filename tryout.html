This is our tryout flier from the current '19-'20 season.



Tryouts consist of two days, the first day we learn a two or three
short routines. The routines are commonly pom, jazz and hip-hop.
The second day consists of going over the routines and trying out
in groups of 3 or 4 people.

During the tryout in groups, you will be asked to showcase some
of your best skills, whether it be turns, jumps or flips, it could be
anything you're good at! Don't be discouraged if you don't have
many special skills. In order to be on the team, all you need is a
basic knowledge of dance and a good work ethic!

What you will need:


Proper jazz shoes, Sneakers, Tight black clothing, Water, Snacks
